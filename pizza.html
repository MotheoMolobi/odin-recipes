Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place warm water, yeast and sugar in bowl
  3. Add flour, oil and salt to yeat mixture
  4. Lest rest for 5 minutes
  5. Turn dough on lightly floured surface
  6. Transfer to pizza pan
  7. Spread crust with pizza sauce and toppings
  8. Bake in pre heated oven
